# Synthesis Framework: From Pain to Product

This framework guides Step 4 of the Gold Mining process: turning qualitative Reddit data into a concrete product concept.

## The Synthesis Process

1. **Clustering**: Group similar pain points together. Identify the "Big 3" frustrations.
2. **Inversion**: Turn each frustration into a feature or benefit.
   - *Pain*: "I hate manually copy-pasting data between X and Y."
   - *Inversion*: "Automated sync between X and Y."
3. **The "Minimum Viable Solution" (MVS)**: What is the smallest tool that solves the most painful 20% of the problem (which usually accounts for 80% of the frustration)?

## Output Structure

For each concept, generate:

- **Product Name**: Catchy and descriptive.
- **One-Sentence Pitch**: "The [X] for [Y] that [Z]."
- **Primary Pain Point**: The specific frustration it solves.
- **The "Magic Moment"**: The first thing a user does that makes them realize the value.
- **Pricing Strategy**: How would this be monetized? (SaaS, one-time, per-use).

## Refinement Questions

- Does this solve a "burning" problem or just a "nice-to-have"?
- Can this be built in 2-4 weeks?
- Is there a clear community (Subreddit) to market this back to?
